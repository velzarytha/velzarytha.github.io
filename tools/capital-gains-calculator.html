Basis matters

A missing cost basis can materially distort the result.

Gain or loss is generally estimated by comparing net sale proceeds with adjusted basis. Supporting documents may be needed when a broker statement reports an incomplete or zero basis.

Documents that may help

  • Purchase confirmations or closing statements
  • Reinvestment and corporate-action records
  • Improvement invoices
  • Prior depreciation schedules
  • Supplemental broker statements
