How it works

Understand the basis before the schedule.

The calculator starts with purchase price and capitalized costs, removes land and salvage value, then applies the business-use percentage. The remaining amount is divided over the recovery period.

What this version includes

  • Land and other non-depreciable amounts
  • Business-use adjustment
  • Full-year or simple monthly proration
  • Year-by-year printable schedule

What it does not determine

It does not decide whether an asset qualifies for a particular tax treatment. Confirm current federal and state rules before using the result on a tax return.
