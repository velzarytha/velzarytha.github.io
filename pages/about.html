About Velzarytha

Useful calculators, clearly presented.

Velzarytha is an independent collection of free tax and financial planning tools built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

The website is designed to make common calculations easier to understand. Calculations run locally in the browser, and the site does not require an account.

Our approach

We aim to show inputs, assumptions, and results clearly. Tools that depend on changing rules should identify their limitations and should not replace official guidance or professional advice.
